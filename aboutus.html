ABOUT THE ORGANIZERS

MountainHacks is organized by a team of passionate developers in the MHHS Hackathon Club, the largest high-school club of hackers in California! Our mission is to provide students with opportunities to show their skills and learn through experience. We accomplish this through our many programs and activities, including our signature event, MountainHacks!
